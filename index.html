Leg Day Workout Routine

Get ready to sweat with this intense leg day workout routine!

Warm-up

  • 5-10 minutes on the treadmill
  • Dynamic stretching (leg swings, lunges, etc.)

Exercise 1: Squats

3 sets of 12 reps

Exercise 2: Leg Press

3 sets of 12 reps

Exercise 3: Lunges

3 sets of 12 reps (per leg)

Cool-down

5-10 minutes of stretching
